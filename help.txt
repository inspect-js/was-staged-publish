Usage:
was-staged-publish <pkg>[@<version>] [--registry <url>]

Determine whether a published npm version was a staged publish (and thus approved by a human passing a 2FA challenge).

`version` may be an exact version or a dist-tag; it defaults to `latest`.
`--registry` defaults to https://registry.npmjs.org.

Exit codes:
  0  staged (for sure)
  1  definitely not staged
  2  unknown (cannot be determined)
  3  fetch error
